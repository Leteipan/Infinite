In our gardening approach we use sustainable practices.

Healthy soil

Healthy soil is the foundation of a healthy garden, here at infineat landscapes we DO NOT use any artificial fertilizers. Through many years of doing gardening, we have realize that the use of fertilizers like DAP and CAN tend to burn plants in the long run hence we adapted use of Organic manure from Chicken, Cow & goat. We also pot our plants from Inhouse made clean and fresh compost. Having implemented this practice we aim to ensure the soil is well aerated, full of Organic matters and rich in NPK (Nitrogen, Potassium & Calcium).for a joyful garden

Cover crops

Cover crops are traditionally planted on farms but they can also be used in gardens. In our pursuit to control evaporation in the garden we create for our Clients, we add on the plant list ground covers that aim to keep the ground moist for longer period, this in return encourage decomposition and formation of organic matters in the Soil

Biological control

Be sure to monitor insect and mite activity in your garden.

Conserve water

Be sure to water your garden at the right time of day.

Native Plants and Trees

Planting native plants and trees is one of the best ways to work with, rather than against, nature. By matching plant species to your particular area you will have plants and trees that take less care and energy and will be healthier than exotic species.Another benefit is that native birds, insects, and other wildlife have evolved with native plant species and are able to use the fruits, nectars and habitat these plants and trees provide.
